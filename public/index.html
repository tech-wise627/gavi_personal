🎫 JR Pass – Your Golden Ticket

¥50,000 / ~$330 USD per adult

What's Covered:

  • All JR Shinkansen (bullet trains) except Nozomi & Mizuho
  • JR local trains in all cities
  • JR Ferry to Miyajima Island
  • Narita Express to/from Tokyo
  • JR buses in select areas
💡 Pro Tip: Activate your pass on Day 1 in Tokyo. The pass runs on consecutive calendar days, not 24-hour periods. Pick up at any major JR station with your exchange order.

Tokyo

4 Nights • May 25–29

🏠 Stay in Shibuya or Shinjuku – Central access, family-friendly, great food options

Day 1 – Shimokitazawa Wander

▼
🌅 Morning
Arrive at Narita/Haneda 🔮 OFFBEAT
Take Narita Express (JR Pass!) to Shibuya. Check into Airbnb and freshen up.
⏱ 2-3 hours 💴 Free with JR Pass
☀️ Afternoon
Shimokitazawa Exploration
Tokyo's coolest bohemian neighbourhood. Vintage shops, indie cafes, street art, and chill vibes. Perfect jet-lag friendly wandering.
⏱ 3-4 hours 💴 Free (shopping extra)
Café lunch at Bear Pond Espresso 🥬 VEG PICK
Famous coffee spot with vegetarian pastries and light bites.
⏱ 1 hour 💴 ¥1,500/person
🌙 Evening
Afuri Ramen Ebisu 🥬 VEG PICK
Famous yuzu shio ramen with vegetarian/vegan options. Light, citrusy, refreshing.
⏱ 1 hour 💴 ¥1,200/bowl

Day 2 – Yanaka Old Town + Nezu Shrine

▼
🌅 Morning
Yanaka Ginza Shopping Street 🔮 OFFBEAT
Step back in time in Tokyo's old-school neighbourhood. Traditional shops, cat statues everywhere, and zero tourists.
⏱ 2 hours 💴 Free
☀️ Afternoon
Nezu Shrine 🔮 OFFBEAT
One of Tokyo's oldest shrines with stunning azalea garden and thousands of mini torii gates (mini Fushimi Inari!).
⏱ 1.5 hours 💴 ¥200 garden entry
Kayaba Coffee 🥬 VEG PICK
100-year-old coffee house in a preserved wooden building. Egg sandwiches and fluffy pancakes.
⏱ 1 hour 💴 ¥1,000/person
🌙 Evening
Ueno Park Sunset Stroll
Beautiful park walk, street performers, and temple hopping as the sun sets.
⏱ 2 hours 💴 Free

Day 3 – Street Go-Kart + Shibuya Sky Night

▼
🌅 Morning
Meiji Shrine
Peaceful forest shrine in the heart of Shibuya. Write wishes on wooden plaques.
⏱ 1.5 hours 💴 Free
☀️ Afternoon
Street Go-Kart Experience 🔮 OFFBEAT
Drive real go-karts through Tokyo streets in costume! Book JAPANKART for the best experience. International license required.
⏱ 2-3 hours 💴 ¥10,000/person
🌙 Evening
Shibuya Sky Observation Deck
360° rooftop views of Tokyo skyline at sunset. Book sunset slot for best experience.
⏱ 1.5 hours 💴 ¥2,200/person
Shibuya Crossing + Dinner 🥬 VEG PICK
Watch the famous crossing from Starbucks, then vegetarian dinner at Ain Soph Journey.
⏱ 2 hours 💴 ¥3,000/person

Day 4 – TeamLab Planets + Odaiba

▼
🌅 Morning
TeamLab Planets
Immersive digital art museum – wade through water, walk through infinity. Book 9-10am slot to avoid crowds.
⏱ 2-3 hours 💴 ¥3,800/person
☀️ Afternoon
Odaiba Island Exploration
Futuristic island with rainbow bridge views, giant Gundam statue, and beach vibes.
⏱ 3-4 hours 💴 Free
DiverCity Food Court 🥬 VEG PICK
Multiple vegetarian options in this mall's food court including curry, udon, and more.
⏱ 1 hour 💴 ¥1,500/person
🌙 Evening
Pack & Prepare for Kyoto
Early Shinkansen tomorrow! Pack bags, organize JR Pass, and rest up.
⏱ Evening 💴 Free
⛩️

Kyoto

3 Nights • May 29 – June 1

🏠 Stay in Gion or Higashiyama – Traditional machiya houses, walking distance to temples

Day 5 – Fushimi Inari Dawn

▼
🌅 Early Morning (5:30am!)
Fushimi Inari at Dawn 🔮 OFFBEAT
Beat ALL the crowds. Arrive 5:30am for magical empty torii gates and sunrise light. Worth the early wake!
⏱ 2-3 hours 💴 Free
☀️ Late Morning
Vermillion Cafe Breakfast 🥬 VEG PICK
Right at Fushimi Inari base. Great coffee and vegetarian breakfast with shrine views.
⏱ 1 hour 💴 ¥1,200/person
☀️ Afternoon
Nap + Check into Kyoto Airbnb
You earned it after that early morning! Rest up in your machiya townhouse.
⏱ 2-3 hours 💴 Free
Gion District Walk
Evening geisha district stroll. Spot maiko (apprentice geisha) on their way to appointments.
⏱ 2 hours 💴 Free
🌙 Evening
Okutan Nanzenji 🥬 VEG PICK
400-year-old tofu restaurant in temple grounds. Traditional yudofu (hot pot tofu) course. BOOK AHEAD!
⏱ 1.5 hours 💴 ¥3,500/person

Day 6 – Arashiyama Offbeat

▼
🌅 Morning
Bamboo Grove (Early!)
Arrive by 7am to experience the grove without crowds. Magical morning light.
⏱ 1 hour 💴 Free
Gio-ji Temple 🔮 OFFBEAT
Hidden moss temple that most tourists miss. Incredibly peaceful thatched-roof temple covered in moss.
⏱ 45 min 💴 ¥300
☀️ Afternoon
Hozugawa River Boat Ride 🔮 OFFBEAT
Traditional wooden boat down the river with boatmen using poles. Stunning gorge scenery.
⏱ 2 hours 💴 ¥4,100/person
Arashiyama Yoshimura 🥬 VEG PICK
Soba noodle restaurant with river views. Ask for vegetarian tempura soba.
⏱ 1 hour 💴 ¥1,500/person
🌙 Evening
Monkey Park Iwatayama
Hilltop park with 120 wild monkeys and panoramic Kyoto views. Fun for kids!
⏱ 1.5 hours 💴 ¥550/person

Day 7 – Nara Day Trip

▼
🌅 Morning
Train to Nara
45-minute JR train from Kyoto. Free with JR Pass!
⏱ 45 min 💴 Free with JR Pass
Nara Park + Deer
Meet the famous bowing deer! Buy deer crackers (shika senbei) and make friends.
⏱ 2 hours 💴 ¥200 for crackers
☀️ Afternoon
Todai-ji Temple
World's largest wooden building with giant bronze Buddha. Mind-blowing scale.
⏱ 1.5 hours 💴 ¥600/person
Naramachi Walking 🔮 OFFBEAT
Traditional merchant district with edo-period houses, craft shops, and hidden cafes.
⏱ 2 hours 💴 Free
🌙 Evening
Return to Kyoto
Relax evening in Kyoto. Explore Pontocho alley for dinner or grab konbini snacks.
⏱ Evening 💴 Varies
☮️

Hiroshima + Miyajima

2 Nights • June 1–3

🏠 Night 1: Hiroshima City • Night 2: Miyajima Ryokan (traditional inn)

Day 8 – Hiroshima City

▼
🌅 Morning
Shinkansen Kyoto → Hiroshima
1.5 hour bullet train ride. Grab ekiben (train bento) for the journey!
⏱ 1.5 hours 💴 Free with JR Pass
Peace Memorial Park
Atomic Bomb Dome, Peace Memorial Museum, Children's Peace Monument. Heavy but essential.
⏱ 3 hours 💴 ¥200 museum
☀️ Afternoon
Shukkeien Garden 🔮 OFFBEAT
Beautiful Edo-period garden that survived the bombing. Peaceful contrast to the morning.
⏱ 1 hour 💴 ¥260
Nagata-ya Vegan Okonomiyaki 🥬 VEG PICK
Hiroshima-style layered okonomiyaki with vegan options available. Local institution.
⏱ 1 hour 💴 ¥1,200/person
🌙 Evening
Hiroshima Night Walk
Stroll along the river, see the Peace Dome lit up at night. Reflect on the day.
⏱ 2 hours 💴 Free

Day 9 – Miyajima Island Magic

▼
🌅 Morning
Ferry to Miyajima
JR Ferry from Miyajimaguchi (free with JR Pass). 10-minute scenic ride.
⏱ 1 hour total 💴 Free with JR Pass
Floating Torii Gate
Iconic vermillion gate in the water. Check tide times – low tide lets you walk to it!
⏱ 1 hour 💴 Free
☀️ Afternoon
Mt. Misen Hike/Ropeway 🔮 OFFBEAT
Sacred mountain with wild deer and monkeys. Take ropeway up, hike down for best views.
⏱ 3-4 hours 💴 ¥1,840 ropeway
🌙 Evening
Check into Iwaso Ryokan 🔮 OFFBEAT
150-year-old traditional inn. Includes kaiseki dinner (request vegetarian), onsen bath, futon sleeping.
⏱ Evening 💴 ~¥35,000/room
🌙 Night
Night Torii Gate View 🔮 OFFBEAT
After day-trippers leave, walk to the lit-up torii gate. Almost no one around. Magical.
⏱ 30 min 💴 Free

Day 10 – Miyajima Sunrise → Osaka

▼
🌅 Very Early Morning (5:30am)
Sunrise at Torii Gate 🔮 OFFBEAT
Wake at 5am, walk to the torii gate for sunrise. You'll likely be alone. Unforgettable.
⏱ 1 hour 💴 Free
🌅 Morning
Ryokan Breakfast + Checkout
Traditional Japanese breakfast included. Pack up and ferry back to mainland.
⏱ 2 hours 💴 Included
☀️ Afternoon
Shinkansen → Osaka
1.5 hour ride to Shin-Osaka. Check into your final Airbnb in Namba or Shinsaibashi.
⏱ 2 hours total 💴 Free with JR Pass
🏯

Osaka

5 Nights • June 3–8

🏠 Stay in Namba or Shinsaibashi – Best food scene, nightlife, and metro access

Day 11 – Shinsekai + Osaka Castle

▼
🌅 Morning
Osaka Castle
Iconic castle with beautiful park. Museum inside shows Osaka history.
⏱ 2-3 hours 💴 ¥600
☀️ Afternoon
Shinsekai District 🔮 OFFBEAT
Retro neon district with Tsutenkaku Tower. Feels like 1960s Japan. Kitschy and fun.
⏱ 2-3 hours 💴 Free (tower ¥900)
Kushikatsu (Veggie Skewers) 🥬 VEG PICK
Shinsekai specialty. Some places have vegetable-only skewers deep fried to perfection.
⏱ 1 hour 💴 ¥1,500/person
🌙 Evening
Spa World
Giant onsen theme park with baths from around the world. Great for families.
⏱ 2-3 hours 💴 ¥1,500/person

Day 12 – Nakazakicho + Kuromon Market

▼
🌅 Morning
Nakazakicho 🔮 OFFBEAT
Osaka's hipster village. Converted old houses into cafes, galleries, and vintage shops. Zero tourists.
⏱ 2-3 hours 💴 Free
Salon de Amanto Cafe 🥬 VEG PICK
Cute retro cafe in a renovated house. Vegetarian lunch plates and great coffee.
⏱ 1 hour 💴 ¥1,200/person
☀️ Afternoon
Kuromon Market
"Osaka's Kitchen" – 170+ stalls of street food. Lots of veggie options including tamagoyaki (egg omelette).
⏱ 2 hours 💴 ¥2,000 grazing
🌙 Evening
Dotonbori Night Walk
Neon wonderland! Glico Running Man, canal reflections, street performers. Peak Osaka energy.
⏱ 2-3 hours 💴 Free

Day 13 – Sumiyoshi Taisha + Amerika-Mura

▼
🌅 Morning
Sumiyoshi Taisha 🔮 OFFBEAT
One of Japan's oldest shrines with unique Sumiyoshi architectural style. Iconic arched bridge.
⏱ 1.5 hours 💴 Free
☀️ Afternoon
Amerika-Mura (Ame-Mura)
Osaka's youth culture hub. Street fashion, record stores, and wild energy. Japanese Harajuku.
⏱ 2-3 hours 💴 Free (shopping extra)
Triangle Park People Watching 🔮 OFFBEAT
The heart of Ame-Mura. Grab taiyaki and watch Osaka's coolest kids hang out.
⏱ 1 hour 💴 ¥300
🌙 Evening
Shinsaibashi Shopping Arcade
600m covered shopping street. Great for souvenirs, fashion, and late-night shopping.
⏱ 2 hours 💴 Varies

Day 14 – Optional: Kobe Day Trip

▼
🌅 Morning
JR to Kobe
30 minutes from Osaka. Beautiful port city with mountain backdrop.
⏱ 30 min 💴 Free with JR Pass
Kitano Ijinkan 🔮 OFFBEAT
District of Western-style houses from the Meiji era. Feels like Europe in Japan.
⏱ 2 hours 💴 ~¥500/house
☀️ Afternoon
Nankinmachi (Chinatown) 🥬 VEG PICK
Japan's oldest Chinatown. Great for vegetarian options – Buddhist veg restaurants here!
⏱ 2 hours 💴 ¥1,500/person
Kobe Harborland
Waterfront area with shopping, ferris wheel, and great views of the port.
⏱ 2 hours 💴 Free

Day 15 – Departure Day

▼
🌅 Morning
Last Konbini Run
Stock up on Japanese snacks, Kit-Kats, and instant noodles for home. Family Mart or 7-Eleven.
⏱ 30 min 💴 Varies
Kansai Airport (KIX)
Take Haruka Express from Tennoji or Nankai from Namba. Arrive 3 hours before flight.
⏱ 1-1.5 hours 💴 ~¥1,500
